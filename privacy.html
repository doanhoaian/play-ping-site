Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 September 2025

Quick summary

  • No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected. No account is required.
  • Android VpnService powers the simulation; processing happens locally on device.
  • Traffic may be temporarily routed through relay servers to keep the VPN alive; no content is logged.
  • Firebase and the PlayPing backend are used for technical diagnostics and push notifications only.
  • The app is available on Android only and is not currently distributed in the EU or the United States.

1. Introduction & scope

PlayPing (Dihaver Tech) allows you to simulate packet loss, latency, bandwidth limits, and disconnects so you can test or train in a safe environment on Android devices. This policy applies to all PlayPing users on Android and does not cover regions where the app is not distributed (the European Union and the United States).

2. Data we process

We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) and we do not require an account.

To keep the service reliable we may process anonymous technical data such as crash reports, performance metrics, device type, Android version, and notification opt-in status. These data points are used to troubleshoot and improve quality, not to identify you and never for behavioural advertising.

3. How we use the data

  • Maintain stability, fix issues, and improve performance.
  • Send update or feature notifications (when you opt in).
  • Analyse technical trends to optimise the experience.

4. How the VPN simulation works

PlayPing relies on Android VpnService to create a virtual connection and apply simulated network conditions.

  • The VPN connection is used solely for simulation purposes.
  • The app does not monitor your browsing activity and does not capture traffic content.
  • Processing happens on device. In some cases, when the VPN is active but no simulation is configured, traffic can be temporarily routed through relay servers to avoid disconnections; those servers do not store content, log browsing, or inspect data.

5. App permissions

PlayPing only asks for permissions that are necessary to operate transparently and lawfully:

Permission Purpose
INTERNET, ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE Connect to the network and inspect state for simulations and updates.
SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW Display floating controls so you can toggle simulations quickly.
FOREGROUND_SERVICE, FOREGROUND_SERVICE_SPECIAL_USE, WAKE_LOCK Keep background services (such as the VPN) stable and prevent Android from stopping them.
POST_NOTIFICATIONS, VIBRATE Deliver status or update notifications and provide haptic feedback.
REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS Reduce the chance that Android throttles critical background services.

PlayPing does not access your messages, contacts, location, microphone, or camera.

6. Servers & processors

Service Purpose Location
Google Firebase Push notifications, crash reporting, analytics limited to technical metrics. Global infrastructure
PlayPing relay servers Maintain VPN connectivity when a simulation is idle; no content logging. Singapore & Vietnam

7. Data retention

Technical diagnostics are kept only as long as necessary to investigate issues or improve product quality, then removed or aggregated.

8. Children's privacy

PlayPing is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a minor has provided data, contact us to delete it.

9. Your choices

  • You can disable optional analytics or notifications inside the app settings.
  • You may request deletion of diagnostic data associated with your device by emailing us (provide logs or device ID so we can locate entries).

10. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date above and publish the new version here. Continuing to use PlayPing means you agree with the updated policy.
